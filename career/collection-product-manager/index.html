COLLECTION PRODUCT MANAGER (W/M/D)

 

ABOUT ROKKU DESIGN

ROKKU DESIGN is a design studio based in Hamburg, Germany. We are specialized in research, design, product development, and production handling for eyewear products. Our clients include brands, manufacturers and retailers. Through a global network of partners, we are able to provide fashionable eyewear at a competitive price.

Founded in 2015, ROKKU DESIGN is a start-up led by two experienced managers who jointly have more than 20 years of experience in the eyewear industry. With a team composed of young creatives and business entrepreneurs we provide our clients with innovative design, professional services and customized product proposals.

RESPONSABILITIES

• Collaboration on projects in the Fielmann 2025 project organization, in particular on the digitization of the value chain for optical and sunglasses
• Collaboration on existing and new project plans and business cases
• Control of strategic development plans and guidelines for the Design & Product Development department and product assortment for E-Commerce as well as recording, detailing, analyzing and optimizing of processes in the Design & Product Development Department and product assortment for E-Commerce
• Recording and analysing strategic framework through the coordination with cross-functional teams
• Contact person for the Senior Collection Product Manager and Head of Design & Product Development as well as for the project organization of all existing and planned processes
• Interface between Design, Content, Marketing, E-Commerce, Purchase and Procurement


WHAT WE EXPECT

• Successfully completed business-related studies (Business Administration, Fashion Management or comparable)
• > 2 years of relevant experience in Product Management (ideally in the areas of fashion or eyewear)
• Confident handling of Microsoft Office and Adobe suite (especially Illustrator) advantageous
• Business level fluency of English
• Additional languages such as Italian advantageous
• Distinct sense of style and interest in fashion, brands and trends
• Independent, committed and structured way of working
• Organizational and coordination skills and strong analytical thinking skills
• Ability to thrive in a high-growth, entrepreneurial and ambiguous environment
• Reliability, conscientiousness and ability to work under pressure
• Outstanding teamworking and team-building skills
• Strong interpersonal skills and excellent written and spoken communication skills


WHAT WE OFFER
