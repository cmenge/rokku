JUNIOR PRODUCT MANAGER (M/F)

 

ABOUT ROKKU DESIGN

ROKKU DESIGN is a design studio based in Hamburg, Germany. We specialize in research, design, product development, and production handling for eyewear products. Our clients include brands, manufacturers and retailers. Through a global network of partners we are able to provide fashionable eyewear at competitive prices.

Founded in 2015 ROKKU DESIGN is a start-up led by two experienced managers who jointly have more than 20 years of experience in the eyewear industry. With a team composed of young creatives and business entrepreneurs we provide our clients with innovative design, professional services and customized product proposals.

ABOUT THE JOB

You will be part of ROKKUs operations team. In this role you will work closely with our Head of Operations and assume responsibility in smooth workflows of operational processes. Besides being in charge of conceptual tasks, such as process organization and optimization of internal workflows, you do color & material research and elaborate design briefings as well as analyses for projects. Furthermore, you bridge the gap between our design team, clients, suppliers and partners by coordinating the communication and handling of deliveries. During your work you have the opportunity to work independently to pursue your own ideas.

WHAT WE EXPECT

• Completed business-related university degree (Bus. Adm., Fashion Management etc.) or comparable qualification
• First relevant practical experience (ideally in fashion, industry or trade)
• Ability to work independently, organizational skills, coordination skills, distinctive intellectual capacity
• Distinctive sense for style and Interest in fashion, brands and trends
• Reliability, conscience, resilience
• Proficiency in the use of MS Office software; basics in Adobe Suite (esp. Illustrator) are of advantage
• Strong command of the English language
• Strong interpersonal skills and distinctive communication skills with words and in writing

WHAT WE OFFER

At ROKKU you will be part of a young and dynamic team. You work independently in close collaboration with the management. Our office is located in the heart of Eppendorf, Hamburg. In the top floor of a historical building we are working in an environment that caters to the needs of creatives.
